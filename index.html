Who I am

My body, my truth

I'm Mazz. I got diagnosed with endometriosis when I was 24. But honestly, it took way too long to get there. Before the diagnosis, it was just me showing up at the ER over and over ‚Äî crazy pain in my lower abdomen and joints, and nobody knowing what to do with me.

In 2022, they convinced me I needed a cystectomy. Turns out I didn't. Since then, it's been one medication after another, and most of them messed with my head more than they helped my body. Now I have anxiety that I didn't have before, and clinical depression that hits out of nowhere.

But I'm not letting this thing take over my whole life. I still want to do amazing things, and I've decided I'm not waiting until I feel "better" to start living. So here I am, writing about it. Because if even one person reads this and feels less alone, it's worth it.
